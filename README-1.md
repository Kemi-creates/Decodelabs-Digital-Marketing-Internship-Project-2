## 📌 Project Overview

This project was completed as part of the **DecodeLabs Industrial Training Kit (Batch 2026)** — a structured digital marketing internship programme.

**Project Goal:** Perform basic keyword research for a website or blog, identify search intent behind each keyword, and suggest relevant content ideas based on findings.

**Deliverable:** A structured keyword research report containing 19 targeted keywords, organised by search intent, difficulty, and content strategy recommendations.

---

## 🎯 Niche Researched

**African Parenting Digital Products**

Specifically, this research targets a blog/website that sells digital products (ebooks and guides) for **African parents of children aged 4–7 years.**

This niche was selected because:
- It addresses a highly specific and underserved audience
- Digital product demand in the Nigerian/African parenting space is growing
- Low keyword competition makes it realistic for a new website to rank on Google

---

## 🧠 Research Methodology

The keyword research followed a structured, 4-step process:

### Step 1 — Define the Audience
Before searching for any keywords, the target audience was clearly defined:
> *African parents (primarily Nigerian) raising children between the ages of 4 and 7, actively looking for parenting guidance, learning resources, and digital tools.*

This ensured every keyword researched was relevant to a real person with a real need.

### Step 2 — Generate Seed Keywords
Broad "seed" keywords were identified first as starting points for deeper research:
- parenting tips
- child development
- how to raise a smart child
- learning activities for kids
- African parenting

### Step 3 — Expand & Analyse Keywords
Each seed keyword was expanded into more specific variations (especially long-tail keywords), then analysed across four dimensions:

| Dimension | What I Looked For |
|---|---|
| **Search Volume** | How many people search this term monthly |
| **Keyword Difficulty** | How hard it is to rank for this term (lower = better for new sites) |
| **Search Intent** | Why the person is searching (to learn, buy, compare, or find a site) |
| **Keyword Type** | Short-tail (broad) vs Long-tail (specific, lower competition) |

### Step 4 — Map Keywords to Content
Every keyword was matched to a specific content idea — whether a blog post, product page, or landing page — so the research directly informs a real content strategy.

### Search Intent Breakdown

- 🔵 **Informational** — User wants to learn something (e.g. *"how to raise a smart child"*)
- 🟣 **Commercial** — User is researching before buying (e.g. *"best parenting ebook"*)
- 🟢 **Transactional** — User is ready to buy or download (e.g. *"download parenting ebook PDF"*)
- 🟡 **Navigational** — User is looking for a specific site or brand (e.g. *"Nigerian parenting blog"*)
